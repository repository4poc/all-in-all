| Feature          | Azure Reserved VM Instance | Azure Savings Plan        | AWS Reserved Instance                | AWS Savings Plans          |
| ---------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------------- |
| Commitment       | Specific VM                | Hourly spend              | Specific instance                    | Hourly spend               |
| Flexibility      | Low                        | High                      | Low (Standard), Medium (Convertible) | Very High                  |
| Term             | 1 or 3 years               | 1 or 3 years              | 1 or 3 years                         | 1 or 3 years               |
| Applies to       | Azure VMs                  | Eligible compute services | Amazon EC2                           | EC2, Lambda, Fargate       |
| Instance changes | Limited                    | Automatic                 | Convertible RI only                  | Automatic                  |
| Regional scope   | Regional or shared         | Automatic                 | Regional or AZ                       | Global (depending on type) |
